Orange Group Company

Powering the future of TV & video

Viaccess-Orca (VO) is a leading provider of data-driven, end-to-end solutions that empower TV operators and service providers to deliver, secure, and monetize viewing experiences on every screen.

From AI-powered personalization and advanced content security to cloud-native OTT platforms and targeted advertising, VO delivers the technology and expertise operators need to stay ahead in a rapidly evolving landscape.

As a subsidiary of Orange Group, VO combines the agility of a specialist technology company with the strength and global reach of one of the world's leading telecommunications operators.
